The Quintes Engine: From Deposit to Yield

Solutions
We don't just stake your tokens; we engineer value. Our protocol automates complex hedging strategies to turn your static assets into a powerful, appreciating instrument.
1. Deposit & Mint

You deposit BTC, ETH, or Stablecoins into the protocol smart contracts. In return, you mint QNT, receiving immediate liquid exposure to our engineered growth token.

2. Collateral Deployment

Your assets don't sit idle. The protocol securely deploys them into Delta-Neutral strategies via institutional custodians (Fireblocks), generating real yield from market volatility without directional risk.

3. Automated Compounding

The yield generated is used to buy back and burn QNT or increase the collateral reserve. This mechanism forces the price of QNT to rise consistently relative to the collateral, targeting ~33% APY.

4. Peg Stability & Arbitrage

Our PegKeepers and arbitrage bots monitor the price 24/7. If QNT deviates from its target growth trajectory, the system automatically rebalances to restore the price.

5. Redemption & Exit

Need liquidity? You can sell QNT on the open market or redeem it directly against the collateral treasury at any time, ensuring you always have access to your funds.

FAQ

Your Quintes Queries, Answered.

How do I earn with Quintes using my BTC, ETH, or Stablecoins?

You securely deposit your assets to mint QNT, our innovative yield-bearing collateralized asset. Your deposited collateral is then partially deployed into sophisticated, Shariah-compliant trading strategies that generate real revenue. You benefit from QNT's engineered value growth and can earn additional QTS rewards for participating.

What is QNT?

QNT is a yield-bearing, collateralized asset you mint against your deposited collateral. It's overcollateralized and engineered for consistent value growth through unique, patented cryptoeconomic mechanisms, supported by yields from deployed collateral. The goal is to provide a reliable way for your crypto holdings to grow steadily.

How does QNT achieve its consistent growth?

QNT is a synthetic token tied to a target price that is programmed to rise steadily, aiming for significant annual growth. This is supported by profits from deploying user collateral into proven, market-neutral hedging strategies that increase the collateral's value. Automated "PegKeepers" and arbitrage ensure the market price stays aligned with this appreciating target.

Is my deposited collateral always safe?

Security is paramount. For every $1 of QNT, we maintain at least $2 of collateral. If collateral values drop, automated partial liquidation restores this ratio. Additionally, reserve funds and our QTS treasury act as backstops. This Automated Proof of Collateral (APoC) system is designed to ensure QNT is always fully backed and redeemable.

How is Quintes Shariah-compliant?

Our trading strategies, investment policies, and mechanisms are reviewed by Shariah scholars to ensure they align with Islamic financial principles, avoiding interest (Riba) and excessive uncertainty (Gharar).

How is Quintes different from past yield protocols like Luna/UST?

The difference is fundamental. Quintes is built on full 200% over-collateralization with diverse assets like BTC, ETH, and stablecoins. Luna/UST was an undercollateralized, algorithmic model reliant on its sister token's price. Our system is designed for long-term stability and solvency, a lesson learned from the failures of past protocols.
